Currently, I am a doctoral candidate in Mathematics at TU Wien without a supervisor, and a Master's student at the Angewandte Wien in the Transdisciplinary Arts (TransArts) Class.

Recently, I was working as an ESR (Early-Stage Researcher) at TU Wien to obtain my Ph.D., funded the EU project called the ARCADES Network.

Before that, I was a Master’s student in Mathematics at Universität Bonn, Germany (2012 – 2015), wherein my interests are in differential Geometry, specifically symplectic Geometry and geometric Mechanics.

Most awe-inspiring for me, are the functionalities and visualities of the cusps in spatialities — this is to say, the bearings and limits of abstract spaces.
