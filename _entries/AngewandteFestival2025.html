»TRETEN«
black film, spray glue, time, dust [30 × 215 cm]
An action leaves its trace on the object upon which it was acting, as the past remains in the present and the present is pushed into the future. Within another, a word appears through collected dust.

Part of the TransArts Class studio show curated by Leon Hösl.
