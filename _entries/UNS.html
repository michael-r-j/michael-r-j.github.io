We come to be through encounters, and we are formed by those across from us. A you appears and is pressed upon by an I: they find an each‑ other in an us.
This work comprises three paper objects: two unique intaglio prints with embossing and one pure embossing. These paper objects are results of an altered printing process, in which each print is simultaneously an embossing.
Three letters (I C H, English I) were cut out of steel, and two letters (D U, English you) were etched into a copper plate with aquatint. The steel letters and the plate were then layered with paper, stacked upon each other, and printed. In this process, steel letters are pushed through a plate, the I (Ich) and you (Du) are pressed together and thusly mutually embossed by each other. Because the plate is deformed with each printing pass, each print is unique.
