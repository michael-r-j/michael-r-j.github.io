As people, we come to be in the intersection of social spaces and through the interplay of personal boundaries. Our selves find their place through the intermingling of vulnerability and trust. How do we navigate those confrontations, both alone and collectively?
A balloon finds itself in the embrace of the doors of the 2nd Floor space. It proposes the act of sitting with unease, to engage with hope, while asking what it means to be vulnerable as a dimension of self definition.

Where does safety end and transgression start?
