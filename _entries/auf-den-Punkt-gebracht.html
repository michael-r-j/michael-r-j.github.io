»Fenster auf den Punkt gebracht«
wooden support, hinges, latches, reüsed windows [90 × 160 × 185 cm]

A collection of two objects with threefold symmetry and chirality. The threefold wooden supports carrying triplets of reüsed building materials that were once used to delineate boundary, placed about a single point on three mutually-perpendicular planes. Each object has a mirrored, chiral twin.

Materials were provided by materialnomaden.
