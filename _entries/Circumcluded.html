Relationship as a coming together of material: the receptive nature of the cotton support becomes the relay of the interaction, taking up the patina of the copper, while inverting the act of penetration unto passivity.
The piece becomes the sum total of the receiving for being given, the supporting for being held, the engaging for being moved.
